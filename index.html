Introduction

Ningbo Science School

Ningbo Science School is an educational school based on honesty, hard work, seriousness and hard work. The school is mainly responsible for the content of scientific education to help students broaden their thinking and explore the scientific knowledge around them.

Purpose

The purpose of Ningbo Science School is to let students get to the bottom of the matter, that is, to find all kinds of laws. Help students explore physics, chemistry, biology and other knowledge. Let the students develop their own exploration ability is the biggest goal of our school.
